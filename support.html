Frequently Asked Questions

How long does domain transfer take?

Most domain transfers complete within 24-48 hours. We'll keep you updated throughout the process and provide all necessary documentation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency through our secure platform partners Sedo and GoDaddy.

Can I use a domain while on a lease-to-own plan?

Yes! You can use the domain for your website, email, and business needs while making payments. Full ownership transfers upon completion of payments.

Do you offer domain appraisals?

Yes, we provide professional domain appraisals with detailed market analysis, comparable sales data, and investment recommendations.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll work with you to find a solution or process a full refund.

How do I make an offer on a domain?

Contact us through our contact form or email with the domain name and your offer. We'll negotiate on your behalf and respond within 48 hours.

Are your domains authentic and legitimate?

All our domains are verified and legitimate. We work exclusively through trusted platforms like Sedo and GoDaddy to ensure authenticity and secure transfers.

What happens if a domain transfer fails?

In the rare event of a transfer failure, we provide full support to resolve the issue. If resolution isn't possible, we offer a complete refund.
