  • Information shared during domain purchase negotiations

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Website usage data and analytics
  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referral sources and search terms used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your domain inquiries and questions
  • Facilitating domain transactions and transfers

  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

Our website may contain links to third-party services:

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience and site functionality
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior

You have the right to:
